Biography

Alphie started his obsession with creating back in 2009 when he was handed his first camera, which set him on a path towards a passion for computing.

Since then, he has graduated from Goldsmiths University of London, where he directed many music videos under his brand "Ochainzs" and even produced his clothing line while completing his university degree.

These days, he is currently running an online website selling high-value goods and collaborating with well-known brands like NIKE, AUDI, and KITH.
